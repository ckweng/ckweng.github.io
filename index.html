I'm an Assistant Professor of Computer Science at Arizona State University.
My interest lies in applied cryptography. I mainly focus on secure multi-party computation and zero-knowledge proofs.
I teach CSE 539 in Spring 2025 -- Learn applied cryptography by solving puzzles.

Perspective students: Please contact me with your CV and a brief self-introduction.
