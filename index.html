About

I'm a fifth year PhD student at Northwestern University. My advisor is Professor Xiao Wang.
My interest lies in Cryptography with a focus on Secure Multi-Party Computation and Zero-Knowledge Proofs.
I'm a recipient of JPMorgan PhD Fellowship.
