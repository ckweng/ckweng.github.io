About

I'm a fourth year PhD student at Computer Science Department of Northwestern University. My interest lies in Cryptography with a focus on Secure Multi-Party Computation and Zero-Knowledge Proofs. My advisor is Xiao Wang.

I received my Bechelor degree from Xidian University in 2019.
